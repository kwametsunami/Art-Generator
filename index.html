Art Generator is a website that generates works of art from the Art Institute of Chicago's collection via their public API. Some titles may not have images due to them being off view for purposes of treatment, preperation for exhibitions, or being loaned out to other institutions. If no image loads, please refresh your browser or click on the button above.
